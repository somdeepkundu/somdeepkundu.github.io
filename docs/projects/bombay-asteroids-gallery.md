# Bombay Asteroids

A retro arcade game where you pilot a spaceship above Mumbai, dodge asteroid waves, and compete on a global leaderboard.

![Bombay Asteroids Showcase](../assets/images/bombay-asteroids-showcase.jpg)

---

## What You Get

- 🎮 **Fast-paced gameplay** — Navigate through 10 iconic Mumbai landmarks (IIT Bombay → Gateway of India)
- 📈 **Progressive difficulty** — More asteroids and faster speeds as you advance
- 🏆 **Real-time leaderboard** — Compete with players worldwide
- 📱 **Works everywhere** — Desktop, tablet, mobile (plays offline too!)

---

## Quick Stats

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Built with** | HTML5, Canvas, JavaScript |
